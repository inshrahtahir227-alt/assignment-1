### 1. Brainstorming Skill
- *What it does:* Generates multiple ideas about any topic in short paragraphs  
- *Replaces:* Manual idea generation and mind-mapping  
- *Time saved:* 20–30 minutes reduced to under 1 minute  
- *Quality improved:* More structured, diverse, and creative ideas  

### 2. Text Summarizer Skill
- *What it does:* Converts long text into short, clear summaries  
- *Replaces:* Reading full articles or writing summaries manually  
- *Time saved:* Reading time reduced by 70–80%  
- *Quality improved:* Key points highlighted, less confusion  

### 3. Short Quiz/Question Generator Skill
- *What it does:* Automatically generates quiz questions from given text  
- *Replaces:* Teachers or students manually making questions  
- *Time saved:* Question creation goes from 15 minutes to seconds  
- *Quality improved:* Questions are focused and relevant to content  

## How to Use These Skills
1. Provide input text or topic  
2. Select the skill (brainstorm, summarize, or quiz)  
3. The AI generates output instantly
